My programming philosophy: Be impatient. Be patient. Write lots and lots of snippets.

I'm going to begin a series detailing some of the principles I use in the process of writing code. I am sure that some of what follows has already been said, but I shamelessly repeat it for my own good and for the good of my readers. Here are some of the topics I plan to cover (in no particular order):

- The importance of being impatient: why and when impatience is a virtue.
- The importance of being patient: why and when patience is a virtue.
- The power of snippets.
- Shedding: solving a problem in it's generic form so that solutions can be reused.
- Code review: opening up your code to the light of day.

These topics, among others, will be the subject of discussion in the weeks to come, so stay tuned!
